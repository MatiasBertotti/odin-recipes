Famous Grandma Salmon

This is a very good recipe from a very old lady that did it with his last efforts of life, because, nothing makes a good old Grandma the happiest Grandma on planet earth than cooking something for his disgracefull small nietos, thats how you say it in spanish because i dont remember in english

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon, drained and flaked
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 small onion, diced
    • SMALL, U HEAR ME?!?
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps for doing the Good ol' Grandma Salmon

    STEP 1

  • Pick through the salmon and remove any bones. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs and add the diced onion, salmon and pepper. Mix thoroughly.

    • STEP 2

  • Shape into 2 ounce patties; about 7 or 8 patties. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat the oil. Fry each patty for 5 minutes on each side or until crispy and golden brown.
